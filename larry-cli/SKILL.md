# Larry's Marketing Experiments

Autonomous marketing experiments for X and TikTok with AI-powered content creation,
hook testing, and growth tracking.

## Posting Schedule
- 7:30 AM, **4:30 PM**, 9:00 PM

## Text Overlay Rules
- Short (≤5 words): 7.5% of image width
- Medium (≤12 words): 6.5%
- Long (>12 words): 5%
- Position: centered at ~28% from top
- Outline: 15% of font size
- Image: 1024×1536 portrait (9:16)

## Diagnostic Framework
| Views | Conversions | Action |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| High  | High        | Scale — make 3 variations immediately |
| High  | Low         | Fix CTA — hook works, conversion path broken |
| Low   | High        | Fix hooks — content converts, needs visibility |
| Low   | Low         | Full reset — try different format/audience |

## Common Mistakes
- Wrong aspect ratio: use 1024×1536 NOT 1536×1024
- Hook style: use reactions ("Wait this is nice??") NOT labels
- Track conversions, not just views
- Wait 2+ hours before connecting TikTok video IDs (API indexing delay)
- Image generation takes 3-9 min — use 10-min timeout
